ABOUT LET'S TALK

Let's Talk is a free online counseling services for our students within the district.

Students have the opportunity to connect with different counselors around the district using this service. Students will also be given the opportunity to have open discussions on daily topics. More services will be availabe throughout the year to help students connect and feel safe.
